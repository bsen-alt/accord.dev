## Typography

We recommend using **system fonts** for optimal performance and accessibility.

Preferred stacks:

```css

  font-family:
    -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu,
    Cantarell, "Open Sans", "Helvetica Neue", sans-serif;
}
```

## Merchandise Guidelines

Planning to feature Accord on swag?

Here are some quick guidelines:

- Stick to approved logos only
- Use primary blue (`#34D199`) as the dominant accent
- Avoid placing logos over complex textures or gradients
- Provide vector files for best printing quality
