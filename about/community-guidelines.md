## Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) to foster an inclusive, harassment-free space.

Key expectations include:

- Being respectful and empathetic
- Giving constructive feedback
- Welcoming newcomers
- Avoiding personal attacks

Violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from participation.

Read the full CoC [here](/accord/code-of-conduct).
